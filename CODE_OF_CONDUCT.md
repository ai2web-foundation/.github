# Code of Conduct

The AI2Web project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: we are committed to a harassment-free, welcoming, and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. Be respectful, assume good intent, and give constructive feedback.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behaviour to **conduct@ai2web.dev**. Reports are handled confidentially. Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, up to and including a permanent ban from project spaces.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant v2.1 applies and is incorporated by reference at the link above.
